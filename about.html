About Ground State

A birthday gift born from an inside joke.

My wife teaches physics at Philippine Science High School. I watched her play Word Solitaire: Associations and thought "I could make a physics version without the annoying ads."

The name comes from our days as physics undergrads at NIP. "Ground state" was our inside joke for that expressionless look after too many problem sets. Not sad, not angry, just... the lowest energy configuration.

Now it's a game where physics terms find their ground state - their proper category.

Why It's Free

I just don't like ads interrupting gameplay. Plus, it's a birthday gift - can't charge my wife for her own present.

For Teachers

Use freely in your classroom. Export tools included. No permission needed.

Open Source

MIT licensed. Fork it, make a Chemistry version, whatever helps students learn.
