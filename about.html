About Ground State

A birthday gift that turned into something useful.

My wife teaches physics at Philippine Science High School. I watched her play Word Solitaire: Associations and thought "I could make a physics version without the annoying ads."

So I built this for her birthday. Then figured other teachers and students might find it useful too.

Why It's Free

I just don't like ads interrupting gameplay. If you can build something better without them, why not?

For Teachers

Use this freely in your classroom. Export tools included (flashcards, study guides). No permission needed.

Open Source

MIT licensed on GitHub. Fork it, improve it, make a Chemistry version - whatever helps students learn.
